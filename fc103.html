Capability Narrative
Our Approach

Maison 103 LLC is a Small Disadvantaged Business (SDB) headquartered in Miramar, Florida, specializing in analytics, management consulting, and program support services for federal and state government agencies.

Our team brings deep expertise in data analytics, performance reporting, business intelligence, and process improvement — translating complex operational data into clear, actionable insights that drive mission outcomes.

We support agencies across the federal landscape including the Department of Veterans Affairs, Department of Health and Human Services, Department of Defense, Department of Homeland Security, and the Department of the Treasury — delivering scalable solutions that adapt to each agency's unique requirements and compliance frameworks.

Maison 103 operates with a commitment to precision, accountability, and measurable results. Whether deployed as a prime contractor or subcontractor, we integrate seamlessly with existing teams to accelerate program delivery and enhance operational performance.
