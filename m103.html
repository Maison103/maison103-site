How We Build

M 103 products are developed through Dynamic Blending (Vineyard, Utah) — a custom formulation lab specializing in premium skincare manufacturing. Every formula is proprietary, designed from scratch to meet the exact performance and ingredient standards of the M 103 brand.

This isn't rebranded stock product. Each formulation went through a full R&D process — from ingredient sourcing to stability testing to lab-verified COA analysis — before moving to production. The result is a line that's truly ours, with complete traceability from lab to shelf.
