The Orchestrator
How Your Day Gets Built
Every morning, your daily check-in triggers a pipeline of 12 engines that run in sequence. The output is one unified protocol for your entire day.
01
Readiness Engine
Analyzes your sleep, energy, stress, and soreness to calculate a readiness score (1–100) and training capacity for the day.
02
Workout Orchestrator
Selects exercises, sets, reps, and weight targets based on your program phase, readiness, volume history, and progression model.
03
Supplement Engine
Builds your supplement stack across five time windows. Adjusts for training day, rest day, and recovery scenarios.
04
Nutrition Engine
Calculates macro targets, meal timing, and hydration goals based on your training load, body composition, and readiness.
05
Lifestyle Engines
Juice recipes, grooming routines, sleep protocol, dog care tasks, home maintenance, vehicle reminders — all generated in parallel.
06
Coach Engine
Summarizes everything into focus areas, warnings, and quick wins. The AI coach layer adds context-aware guidance using your full history.
Cross-Domain Intelligence
Everything Talks to Everything
🍷
Alcohol Recovery Protocol
Log that you drank last night and every engine responds. Water targets increase, supplements adjust for liver support, workout intensity scales down, nutrition recalibrates for recovery, grooming accounts for dehydration. One input. System-wide adaptation.
🤖
AI Coach — Claude Powered
Three coaching styles: motivational, strict, or technical. The coach has full access to your readiness history, training trends, sleep patterns, nutrition adherence, and program context. Ask it anything — it knows your data.
📈
Readiness-Driven Autoregulation
Your daily check-in generates a readiness score from sleep quality, fatigue, soreness, stress, and energy. Low readiness automatically reduces training volume, adjusts nutrition targets, and modifies recovery protocols.
🩸
Bloodwork Intelligence
Upload your lab results and the system maps biomarkers to supplement recommendations, flags nutritional deficiencies, and adjusts protocols based on your actual health data — not guesses.
📊
Predictive Analytics
Injury risk scoring, overtraining detection, plateau prediction, optimal deload timing, and goal ETAs with confidence scores. The system warns you before problems happen, not after.
📷
Body Composition Tracking
Progress photos with side-by-side comparison, weight trends, body measurements over time, and visual timeline of your transformation across weeks and months.

Predictive Models
Know Before It Happens
⚠️
Injury Risk
Composite score from volume spikes, sleep deficit, readiness trends, and fatigue accumulation. Alerts before you push too far.
🔥
Overtraining Detection
Monitors weekly volume vs. your rolling average, combined with sleep and readiness data. Flags when accumulated stress exceeds recovery capacity.
🕑
Deload Timing
Tracks fatigue accumulation across training blocks and recommends optimal deload windows based on your individual recovery patterns.
🎯
Goal ETA
Projects when you'll hit your target lifts based on your progression curve, with confidence scores and plateau early warnings.
