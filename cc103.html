Our Approach
How We Work

Maison 103 LLC brings the same analytical discipline and structured methodology we apply in federal consulting to the commercial sector — without the bureaucracy.

We work with organizations that have outgrown spreadsheets and gut-feel decision-making but aren't ready for (or don't need) a Big Four engagement. Our focus is practical analytics, clear strategy, and measurable outcomes — not 200-page decks that collect dust.

Every engagement starts with your data and your goals. We build dashboards, reporting frameworks, and operational playbooks that your team can actually use — then we help you execute against them. Whether it's a 4-week sprint or a 6-month transformation, the work is scoped to deliver results, not billable hours.

We operate with a commitment to precision, transparency, and speed. No black boxes. No vendor lock-in. Just clear thinking applied to your hardest operational problems.
